some other fun attributes include :

  • width and height : you have an idea of what these do
  • autoplay : starts playing right away (plz dont do this)
  • loop : loops a video. use sparingly
  • muted : plays video with no sound
  • poster: supply an image file's url and it will be displayed before the video plays. like a billboard
  • preload : used for buffering large files. valid parameters are 'none' (no preloading), 'auto' (buffer media file), or 'metadata' (only buffers metadata)

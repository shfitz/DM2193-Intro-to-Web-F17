The video tag has a couple parameters. the 'src' attribue is the source link to your video. The 'controls' flag turns on the browser's native control interface for video (play, pause, etc).

The p element inside the video tags is called fallback content. it's something that older browsers will still be able to render, even if they cant show the video (thouth it might still be a nice thing to offer a link somewhere to dl the contentn).

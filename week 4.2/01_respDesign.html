Media Queries

A component of responsive web design!

The viewport is the visible area of a webpage. This size can and will change depending on the device (phone, tablet, etc) and size of a browser window.

the 'meta' element gives the browser instructions on how to render the page. Start including the following in your pages :

<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">

"width=device-width" sets the width of the page to the screen-width of the device. "initial-scale=1.0" sets the zoom level of the page

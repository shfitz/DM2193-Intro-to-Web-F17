HAML classes and IDs

you can declare classes and ids like other attributes, but they need to be used a little differently. instead of (), use a . or # right after the element. You can mix and match as needed.


%p.lede
%p#content
%p#content.lede

If you're applying a class or id t a div, you get to save even more keystrokes.


.container
.container.sweet
#root.container

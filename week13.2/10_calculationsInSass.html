What the math?!

computation

Sass also has the ability to do calculations like addition, subtraction, division, multiplication. When you're using units, the first number should have the value attached. 



width: 40px + 6
width: 40px - 6
width: 40px * 6
width: 40px % 6
width: (40px / 10) /* division requires () */
 
$grid: 16
$column: 40px
$gutter: 20px
$container: ($column * $grid) + ($gutter * $grid)

width: $container

for loops

Useful fr generating a bunch of related classes or styles at once 

@for $col from 1 to 6
  .col-#{$col}
    width: 40px * $col

while loops

you can also do while loops

$heading: 1
@while $heading <= 6
  h#{$heading}
    font-size: 2em - ($heading * .25em)
  $heading: $heading + 1

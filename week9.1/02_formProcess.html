Forms part 2

labels

take a look at this form. One of the things you (hopefully!) notice is that there's a label tag in there now. This, intuitively, adds a label to a form input. makes life easier for the user! Note how the for attribute matches the input name. This name is important. It's part of what you're sending.

there's also a type attribute in there, browsers will render these differently.
